## FACTION ARCHITECTURE — OVERVIEW

Seven factions operate in the CITY_OF_LIGHT build. For Phase 1 purposes, they are divided into:

**Primary factions** (full resource pool, permanent zone presence):
- Jiangshi Syndicate (documentation, binding, Stage architecture)
- Eastwind Owls (observation, archival, fieldwork)
- Shell Parliament / Heikegani Crustocrats (financial, debt, liability)
- Ichthyosapiens (theological reclamation, historical architecture)

**Secondary factions** (limited resource pool, zone-conditional presence):
- Yōkai Joint Operation (hospitality, brokerage, neutral-space enforcement)
- Kuroshio Arachnids (web observation, node tracking, coast-bounded)
- Cephalopod Collective (partial infestation, signal routing, Osaka-bounded)

## PRIMARY FACTION: JIANGSHI SYNDICATE

**Primary zone:** detroit_school (Room 204 — permanent documentation staging)
**Secondary zones:** detroit_apartment (exterior observation), bacon_estate (documentation claim on public activities, hospitality-constrained)

**Starting resource pool:**
- *Documentation capacity:* 3 active Camera Ops (1 deployed, 2 in reserve). Deployment capacity: 2 simultaneous zones.
- *Witness pool:* 47 documented witnesses at Season 1 finale (witness count: 47,000 → 340,000 → 2.1M platform). Secondary subjects: 12 pre-bound at Stage 1 or below.
- *Binding coefficient:* Currently -1.7 (post-footage publication; directed observation architecture failed). Recovery trajectory: unknown. Stillness Council Session #4 required before recovery can be modeled.
- *Financial resources:* Production budget (internal; not Shell Parliament-mediated). Sufficient for 18-month operational extension without external funding.
- *Intelligence:* Full access to their own documentation archive. Read access to publicly filed Eastwind records. No access to sealed Eastwind records.

**Operational capacity:**
- Can deploy documentation equipment in any zone that is not under active hospitality clause
- Can initiate Stage 1 contact (documentation request) with any non-hospitality-protected individual
- Cannot advance to Stage 2 (archival footage acquisition) without existing Stage 1 material on the subject
- Cannot advance Stage 3–5 without the Camera Op's participation (current architecture; see Attempt Five operational memo)

**Expansion conditions:**
- Binding coefficient must return to positive before Stage 5 can advance
- Recovery path: Stillness Council must determine whether Stage 5 can proceed under Attempt Five architecture (oscillation tracking, Camera Op as witness) or whether the architecture must be rebuilt
- Alternative recovery: subject voluntarily completes Stage 3-alt — "what I'm trying to do is—" — on camera without Syndicate management. This advances Stage 5 but may not produce a binding that the Syndicate controls.

**Constraint:**
- Camera Op is no longer a Syndicate instrument. Camera Op operates independently. Syndicate cannot direct Camera Op behavior. Syndicate can be present when Camera Op is present.
- Protagonist B: Stage 1 documented but consent architecture is contested. Stillness Council must rule on whether involuntary Stage 1 documentation is valid before Protagonist B's binding can be advanced.

## PRIMARY FACTION: EASTWIND OWLS

**Primary zone:** cairngorms_library (archive + field operations headquarters)
**Secondary zones:** detroit_school (Edinburgh Exchange Student — field operative), detroit_apartment (historical observation, Moor-series field station), all other zones (observation rights, read-only)

**Starting resource pool:**
- *Archival depth:* Full Tyler archive (TYLER-001 through TYLER-022, plus recent additions including TYLER-003 annual composite and SUBSCRIBER-001). Oldest active archive in the current cycle.
- *Field operatives:* 3 active (Moor-series); 1 embedded (Edinburgh Exchange Student, operating in detroit_school zone)
- *Observation coverage:* Visual monitoring capability in all eight Phase 0 zones. No physical presence in osaka zones (observation is Moor-remote, not in-field for those zones).
- *Intelligence:* Most complete Tyler record in faction possession. SUBSCRIBER-001 (sealed, identity redacted). MIGRANT-001 (the gap file; SM-1978-X cross-reference). Pre-TIDES-001 records (Emily Springerton age 17 event; LOG EXTRACTION ATTAINED).
- *Financial resources:* Non-monetary (Eastwind operates on observation rights and archival access agreements, not currency). Shell Parliament has a reciprocal information exchange arrangement.

**Operational capacity:**
- Can file and retrieve any entry in the archive
- Can deploy field observers to any zone with observation rights (all eight)
- Cannot deploy interventionist operatives (Eastwind doctrine: document, do not intervene)
- Edinburgh Exchange Student: semi-autonomous; can receive briefings and file reports; cannot receive direct orders that would compromise her embedded position

**Expansion conditions:**
- Archive depth increases as events produce new filed entries (continuous; no limit)
- Field operative count requires Cataloguer authorization for new deployments
- The Cataloguer has been considering authorizing a Moor-class operative for osaka_night for 40 years; has not yet filed the authorization

**Constraint:**
- Non-interventionist doctrine is absolute. Eastwind observes. They do not act on what they observe except to document it. This has produced exactly one deviation in the archive's history: the Exchange Student filing reports in real time and relaying them to Protagonist A. The Cataloguer has a footnote about whether this constitutes intervention. The footnote has 8 sub-footnotes. No determination has been reached.

## PRIMARY FACTION: SHELL PARLIAMENT / HEIKEGANI CRUSTOCRATS

**Primary zone:** osaka_underport (Parliament hall, ledger room, debt windows)
**Secondary zones:** any zone where an outstanding debt account is being managed (portable; follows the debt, not the geography)

**Starting resource pool:**
- *Active accounts:* 847 outstanding debt files. Tier 1 (major assets): SM-1620-T (Tyler, primary; unresolved); SM-1623-M (Cube custody chain gap, open; accruing). Tier 2 (standard): SM-1978-X (senior official, sealed; 17 reviews). New: PROTAGONIST-A (pending; 340 shell-marks; co-debtor on SM-1620-T).
- *Collection operatives:* 12 active. Kei: currently designated as advisory operative for Protagonist A account. Status: deviated from protocol once (paid the account deviation fee; reset to square one; not disciplined further; internal note says "deviation produced more information than standard protocol would have").
- *Financial depth:* The Shell Parliament's resource base is the oldest continuously operating financial institution in the current cycle. They predate modern banking. They do not convert to fiat currency. Shell-marks are the unit of account.
- *Intelligence:* The ledger is the intelligence. Every financial transaction in the seven-faction system eventually leaves a trace in the Shell Parliament's records. The ledger is the most complete secondary archive of faction activity in existence.

**Operational capacity:**
- Can file and prosecute debt claims against any entity with outstanding balances
- Can assign collection operatives to any zone (portable mandate)
- Can invoke the liability architecture against Bacon's account for any Cube-adjacent events
- Cannot conduct operational activity inside a Yōkai hospitality clause zone (treaty constraint)

**Expansion conditions:**
- SM-1623-M (Cube custody chain gap) accrues interest until resolved. The gap has been accruing since 1623. At the current rate, the amount is classified as "functionally infinite within standard accounting frameworks." The Parliament has a special ledger category for this.
- PROTAGONIST-A account: collection will advance from advisory to formal demand when intent is established. Intent is the loading factor.

**Constraint:**
- The hospitality clause constraint is absolute. The Yōkai-Crustocrat treaty is older than both factions' current operational frameworks. It is load-bearing for the neutral-space architecture of the entire seven-faction system. Violating it would produce consequences the Parliament has not modeled because the Parliament has never needed to model them.

## PRIMARY FACTION: ICHTHYOSAPIENS

**Primary zone:** vatican_corridors (Atlantis archive, Council chamber)
**Secondary zones:** any zone where a Papal cross-reference agreement is active (osaka_underport by treaty; bacon_estate by invitation — the brine shrimp hospitality was an Ichthyosapien clause invocation)

**Starting resource pool:**
- *Theological reclamation record:* 14,000 years of contested migration records. The Atlantis branch — the most load-bearing contested branch in current active factions. If the Subscriber's plan executes, this record is the primary casualty.
- *Operatives:* Monsignor Carvalho (field operations, primary contact); two archival consultants (support); seven Council members (strategy, non-field).
- *Intelligence:* Complete knowledge of Subscriber contact history since 1743 (same as Bacon's, independently verified). Internal record of all eleven Atlantis branch contacts with current-cycle entities.
- *Financial resources:* Not Shell Parliament-mediated. Independent. Substantial.

**Operational capacity:**
- Can deploy Carvalho-class operatives to any zone with Papal cross-reference agreement
- Can invoke Ichthyosapien theological hospitality (brine shrimp clause) at any threshold
- Can share intelligence with other factions under the Cross-Faction Emergency Protocol (invoked when the Subscriber's plan threatens multiple faction archives simultaneously — current status: protocol active since Carvalho's briefing of the Exchange Student)

**Expansion conditions:**
- Cross-Faction Emergency Protocol enables Ichthyosapiens to reach out to factions they would not normally contact. Currently in progress: negotiations with Eastwind Owls (the Cataloguer received a brief; filed it; has not yet responded) and Shell Parliament (Carvalho has submitted a Cross-Reference Expansion request).
- Council authorization required for any direct operational disclosure to non-Ichthyosapien parties. Carvalho has been acting on implicit Council authorization; formal vote is scheduled.

## SECONDARY FACTION: YŌKAI JOINT OPERATION

**Zone presence:** Any zone where a Yōkai operative establishes a Hospitality anchor point. Currently: osaka_night (tanuki's convenience store), bacon_estate (permanent invitation standing), detroit_school (informal — the tanuki has attended two school-adjacent events under observation-only status).

**Starting resource pool:**
- *Hospitality capacity:* Can invoke the hospitality clause at any established anchor point. Anchor point establishment: threshold + food + living organism + verbal invocation. Time to establish: under 60 seconds.
- *Operative count:* 3 confirmed active (tanuki primary; two others unconfirmed in field reports). The Joint Operation does not publish its roster.
- *Intelligence:* Brokerage-grade. The Joint Operation knows who has talked to whom and who owes what because the hospitality clause requires everyone who invokes it to be trackable. The information is not shared. It is held. It accrues.

**Operational capacity:**
- Neutral-space enforcement within active hospitality zones (no faction operations)
- Voluntary disclosure of operational rules to faction representatives (as the tanuki did with Protagonist A; this is anomalous; logged)
- Cannot take offensive action against any entity under any hospitality clause

**Zone presence:** osaka_night (primary), osaka_underport (partial, through substrate overlap), partial-host individuals in any zone
