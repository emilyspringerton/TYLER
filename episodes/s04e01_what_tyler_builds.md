## COLD OPEN

Handheld footage. A Detroit street in early morning — the same street that appeared in S01E01, same camera angle. Day has broken but barely.

Tyler walks into frame from the left.

He is wearing different clothes than S03E06. Not dramatically different — still his clothes, still the same posture — but different. He has been somewhere. The footage catches this before the Camera Op does.

He looks at the tree.

He stands there for approximately forty seconds. The Camera Op does not speak. The camera holds on his face. He is not performing recognition. He is doing something more specific: comparing. He has seen this tree from 1952. He is checking whether it matches the photographs he brought back.

It does.

He turns to the camera.

**TYLER:** "I'm back."

Two-second pause.

**TYLER:** "I have photographs."

He reaches into his coat pocket and produces a small stack of photographs. Black-and-white. He holds them up — not for the camera, not performatively, but the way someone shows a friend something they've been carrying for a while.

**TYLER:** "The tree was smaller."

The Camera Op's hand appears in frame — she reaches out. He gives her the photographs.

She looks at them. Her voice when she speaks is steady but not casual.

**CAMERA OP:** "You're back."

**TYLER:** "I said that."

**CAMERA OP:** "I know. I'm saying it too."

Title card.

**TYLER**
*Season 4: "What Tyler Builds"*

## EPISODE STRUCTURE

**Day 76, late morning — Detroit, the tree**

Tyler and the Camera Op walk. She has the photographs. He answers the question she hasn't asked yet.

**TYLER:** "1952 was good. I would tell you more but I think some of it should stay there."

**CAMERA OP:** "What did you keep?"

He thinks about this — genuinely, not performing thought.

**TYLER:** "The photographs. The knowledge that I could stay there if I wanted to and I didn't. And—" he pauses, "—I figured out what I want to build."

The Camera Op stops walking. He doesn't — he takes two more steps and then stops, because she stopped.

**CAMERA OP:** "What is it?"

**TYLER:** "I don't know yet. I know what it does. I don't know what it is yet."

**CAMERA OP:** "What does it do?"

Long pause. This is not a Stage 4 pause, not a Stage 5 pause — this is a different category. The pause of someone who has been rehearsing an answer for months and is deciding whether to say it out loud yet.

**TYLER:** "It lets people be in the same place as something that happened. Not in a recording. In the actual place. Except the actual place is also now." He looks at her. "That's not precise."

**CAMERA OP:** "It's pretty precise."

**TYLER:** "You've seen the room behind the table."

**CAMERA OP:** "Yes."

**TYLER:** "That's the shape of it. That's not the thing, but that's the shape."

---

**Day 76, afternoon — Bacon's building, the round table**

Tyler arrives. Bacon is at the table. Emily Springerton is across from him with a notebook open and a pen in her hand.

Tyler stops in the doorway.

**TYLER:** "You're already here."

**EMILY:** "I got here this morning." She doesn't look up from the notebook. "I've been mapping it."

**TYLER:** "Mapping what?"

**EMILY:** *(looks up)* "What you're going to need."

Beat.

**TYLER:** "I don't know what I'm going to need yet."

**EMILY:** "I know." She turns the notebook so he can see it. A dependency tree, starting from a node labeled WHAT TYLER BUILDS (UNKNOWN). "I started with the things I know you'll need regardless of what it turns out to be, and worked from there."

Bacon refills Tyler's cup before Tyler sits down. Tyler sits down. He looks at the map.

**TYLER:** "This is the one you had in S03E04."

**EMILY:** "That one was 37 nodes. This one is—" she counts, "—43."

**TYLER:** "What changed?"

**EMILY:** "You came back."

The Camera Op is in the doorway. This scene is being filmed. The Camera Op has not moved to enter the room. She is filming from the threshold, the way she used to film from the van.

This time it is not surveillance. It is witness. The distinction is in the footage.

---

**Day 76, late afternoon — the room behind the table**

Tyler goes into the room alone. The Camera Op does not follow. Emily Springerton does not follow. Bacon stays at the table.

The Camera Op films the closed door for forty seconds.

**CAMERA OP:** *(quietly, not to anyone in particular)* "He's been in there before."

**EMILY:** *(from across the table)* "What's it like?"

**CAMERA OP:** "It looks like a room."

**EMILY:** "What does it feel like?"

Pause.

**CAMERA OP:** "Like after something complicated has already happened and the only thing left to figure out is what to do with it."

Emily writes something in the notebook.

**EMILY:** "That's going in the dependency map."

---

**Day 77, morning — the building, hallway**

Tyler emerges. He has been in the room since late afternoon of Day 76. Fourteen hours. No one has documented this period except for the Camera Op's shot of the closed door.

He looks like someone who has been working, not like someone who has been sleeping.

**TYLER:** "I need a list of things."

**CAMERA OP:** "Emily already made one."

He almost smiles.

**TYLER:** "Of course she did."

He walks down the hall to where Emily is already at the table with coffee. She slides the notebook across to him.

He reads the dependency map. For three minutes. Emily does not explain it; she waits.

**TYLER:** "The node labeled 'primary material.'"

**EMILY:** "That's the one I couldn't specify without knowing what you're building."

**TYLER:** "It's light." He slides the notebook back. "Not as a metaphor. Actual light. Something that makes a place navigable in the dark." He looks at her. "Can you work with that?"

**EMILY:** *(already writing)* "Yes."

---

**Day 77, midday — the Exchange Student, arriving**

The Exchange Student arrives at Bacon's building. She has not been here before. She has a tote bag, a phone, and the file folder — CEDAR-4's folder, the one she's had since Tides Book 1.

She knocks.

Bacon answers.

**EXCHANGE STUDENT:** "I'm looking for Emily Springerton."

**BACON:** *(steps back to let her in)* "She's at the table."

The Exchange Student enters Bacon's building for the first time. She does not yet know what kind of building this is. She knows it is a place where Emily works. She knows she is here because she looked up the address from the notebook Emily left at the architecture session and couldn't figure out why she needed to come here, and came anyway.

Emily looks up when she enters.

**EMILY:** "You brought the file."

**EXCHANGE STUDENT:** *(sits)* "You knew I had it?"

**EMILY:** "I recognized the folder from a photograph of CEDAR-4's last session. The photograph was in the Tides Book 3 archive." She pauses. "I've been waiting for whoever had it to show up. I thought it would take longer."

**EXCHANGE STUDENT:** "What is CEDAR?"

**EMILY:** "I'll explain it, but first—" she slides the architecture map across "—do any of these nodes look familiar to you?"

The Exchange Student looks at the map. She is quiet for a moment.

**EXCHANGE STUDENT:** "This one." She touches a node labeled *material supply chain*. "I've been tracking this without knowing what it was called."

Emily does not look surprised. She looks like someone whose dependency map has just confirmed a node it needed confirmed.

**EMILY:** "I know. That's why the node is already on the map."
