**[SA]:** Operator-Tenn. Attempt Three.

**[OPERATOR-TENN]:** Attempt Three. Camera Op rotation. Seven successive Camera Ops, on the theory that one of them would produce a documentation relationship that felt organic rather than procedural. He tolerated six. He ignored the seventh entirely. None produced Stage 3 material.

*(beat, per minutes: the speaker requested a moment)*

**[SA]:** No. It is the only part of the doctrine that survived contact with him. He keeps small debts. We keep everything. He may be the only entity in the record who understands why we cannot throw anything away, and I do not intend to ask him, and he would not finish the answer.
