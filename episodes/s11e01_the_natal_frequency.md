## COLD OPEN

**EXT. DRAA VALLEY, MOROCCO — PRE-DAWN**

*The Draa River moves south. The pre-dawn light is specific — not dark, not light, the color the sky makes when it's deciding. Camera Op is standing at the edge of a palmery. She has been here since 3 AM.*

*She does not have a camera. She has a notebook and a pen and the mechanism in her bag, turned off.*

*She turns the mechanism on at 4:17 AM.*

*It reads: 28.7 Hz.*

*She has already been hearing it for an hour and nineteen minutes.*

**CAMERA OP (V.O.)**
*(sealed log, Entry 89)*
I heard it before I turned on the reader. I've heard 28.7 Hz in the archive — Tyler documented it at every network site, going back. I know what it sounds like from the transcriptions.

*(pause)*

I didn't know I could hear it directly. I thought it was instrument-range. Below threshold. The documentation always said "mechanism reading" not "audible frequency." I assumed audible meant human-audible and this wasn't.

*(pause)*

It is. I can hear it. I have been hearing it since I arrived at this site at 3 AM.

*(pause)*

Entry 89. Draa Valley. The river is behind me. The palmery extends for two kilometers toward the old ksar. The mechanism confirms 28.7 Hz. The mechanism confirmed what I already knew.

*(pause)*

This is the second thing that has happened in this practice that I didn't expect. The first was Ahmad's sentence closing. The second is this: I can hear the frequency without equipment.

*(pause)*

Dr. Hassan said that's what al-idrak al-muttasil produces. He said I would learn things Tyler couldn't tell me because Tyler doesn't know how to say them. Maybe this is one of those things.

*(pause)*

I don't know what site this is yet. It's a palmery on the Draa River, south of Ouarzazate, at a bend where the river slows. I came here because I was following the archive's trail of southern Morocco references and this bend appeared in a margin note in Ahmad's Volume III that I've been sitting with for six weeks. Ahmad wrote: *"the palm water slows at the old crossing — three days south of Marrakech, the traveler knows the frequency of the road by the sound of the date palms at dawn."*

*(pause)*

That's as close to a site description as Ahmad ever wrote. I recognized it when I found the valley on the second day. I came here before dawn because that's when Ahmad said the frequency of the road was audible.

*(pause)*

He was right. He's always right. He just can't tell you what he means until you're standing where he was standing.

*(pause)*

I am the second practitioner. I found the site by reading the margin note correctly.

*(pause)*

DRAA-001.

## ACT ONE

**EXT. DRAA VALLEY — DRAA-001 — DAWN**

*Camera Op sits with the mechanism until sunrise. The reading is stable: 28.7 Hz, continuous. No other Goetia activations. Just the baseline.*

*At 6:45 AM, Dr. Hassan arrives with tea.*

**DR. HASSAN**
*(looking at the mechanism display)*
28.7 Hz.

**CAMERA OP**
Since before I turned it on.

**DR. HASSAN**
*(sitting down)*
You heard it.

**CAMERA OP**
Yes.

**DR. HASSAN**
How long?

**CAMERA OP**
I arrived at 3 AM. I heard it immediately. I turned the mechanism on at 4:17 to confirm.

*Dr. Hassan looks at the mechanism. Then at the palmery. Then at the river bend.*

**DR. HASSAN**
Ahmad described this site in Volume III margin. The palm water crossing.

**CAMERA OP**
Yes.

**DR. HASSAN**
He did not describe what made it worth describing. He only described the time: dawn. And the frequency: "the frequency of the road."

**CAMERA OP**
He couldn't describe what he heard without the instrument vocabulary to describe it. He had the practice but not the frequency tables. Tyler had the frequency tables but not Ahmad's manuscript.

*(pause)*

I have both.

**DR. HASSAN**
*(quietly)*
You are the only person in history who has both.

*(a long pause)*

**CAMERA OP**
Why this site? In the whole Draa Valley, why this bend?

**DR. HASSAN**
What does the archive say?

**CAMERA OP**
Tyler never came here. It's not in any of his documented sites. His southern Morocco trajectory went cave-to-Marrakech directly in December 1119. He didn't come south.

**DR. HASSAN**
Then this site is not Tyler's.

**CAMERA OP**
*(looking at the mechanism)*
No.

*(pause)*

It's mine.

## ACT TWO

**EXT. DRAA-001 — MID-MORNING**

*Camera Op is reading at the site. Mechanism still running. The palmery in full daylight is less mysterious but no less specific — the particular angle of the river bend, the way the palms crowd to the water side, the quality of the light on the sand track that runs along the bank.*

*Jiangshi approaches.*

**JIANGSHI**
I've been reviewing the 28.7 Hz records. Every previous instance is associated with a site Tyler visited or a subject present at a Tyler-visited site.

**CAMERA OP**
*(without looking up from her notebook)*
And this site?

**JIANGSHI**
No Tyler association. But the frequency is identical. Same amplitude as the strongest sites in the network.

*(a pause)*

I think the frequency isn't Tyler's. I think the frequency is the network's — the 28.7 Hz is the resonance frequency of wherever the archive is being held.

**CAMERA OP**
And the archive is being held—

**JIANGSHI**
By whoever is doing the holding. Tyler held it for nine centuries. You have been holding it for nine months.

*(pause)*

You're the practitioner now. The network responds to the practitioner.

*(a long pause)*

**CAMERA OP**
So DRAA-001 reads 28.7 Hz because I'm here.

**JIANGSHI**
Because you're here, and because you came here following Ahmad's margin note, and because the practice of continuous perception produces a frequency signature at sites where it's actively in use.

*(pause)*

Tyler generated 28.7 Hz at sites because Tyler was doing al-idrak al-muttasil at those sites. You're generating it here because you are doing al-idrak al-muttasil here.

*(very quietly)*

The frequency is the practice. It's not Tyler's frequency. It's the practice's frequency.

**CAMERA OP**
*(looking up)*
But Tyler's cardiac baseline —

**JIANGSHI**
Tyler's cardiac baseline is 28.7 Hz because Tyler has been practicing al-idrak al-muttasil for nine centuries. His body calibrated to the frequency of the practice. It's in his physiology now.

*(a pause)*

And Emily Springerton shares his natal chart. Which means she generates the same frequency from the same configuration. She never needed to practice for nine centuries. The natal chart already generates the right frequency.

*(another pause)*

The Camera Op's natal chart receives the frequency. That's what the Capricorn/Cancer axis does when it encounters the 28.7 Hz signal: locks to it.

**CAMERA OP**
I'm receiving Tyler and Emily.

**JIANGSHI**
You are receiving the practice and everything that generates the practice. You have always been receiving it. You are now also generating it.

*(long pause)*

You are the second practitioner. After nine months, you're also beginning to generate the frequency. Your body is calibrating.

*(a pause)*

You are going to be generating 28.7 Hz continuously in ten years. Maybe sooner.

*(Camera Op looks at the mechanism reading. Back at the palmery. Back at the mechanism.)*

**CAMERA OP**
*(quietly)*
Entry 89, addendum: Jiangshi thinks I'm calibrating.

*(pause)*

I think she's right.

Entry 89 closed. DRAA-001: open.*
