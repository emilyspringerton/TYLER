## SCENE ONE — THE FOLD

```
EXT. SOMEWHERE — SIX WEEKS, COMPRESSED TO FOUR SECONDS
```

A folded shape on a road shoulder. Tyler sits near it, not doing anything in particular. Six weeks
pass in a single unbroken shot — the light cycles, his position doesn't. On the seventh week the
shape unfolds. It's the Donkey. Neither of them reacts like six weeks is a strange amount of time
to have just happened.

**DONKEY (TEXT CARD, no VO)**
Sorry. Wasn't needed yet.

**TYLER**
I noticed.

## SCENE THREE — THE DIAGNOSTIC

```
INT. A GARAGE, CHROME EVERYWHERE — CONTINUOUS
```

The Unicorn runs a scanner over Tyler, head to foot. The readout: a single word, blinking,
INCONCLUSIVE. The Unicorn stares at it longer than the joke requires.

**UNICORN (TEXT CARD)**
That's not a result. That's a refusal to give me one.

**TYLER**
Some things don't resolve. Doesn't mean they're broken.

The Unicorn pockets the scanner instead of discarding it — the decision to keep running it, made
on camera, wordless.

## SCENE FOUR — MID-PIANO

```
INT. A ROOM WITH A PIANO IN IT, FOR NO ESTABLISHED REASON — CONTINUOUS
```

The Ghost, playing. Tyler enters, doesn't announce himself, doesn't get announced to. The Ghost
doesn't stop playing. Four bars pass before either of them acknowledges the other, and the
acknowledgment is just proximity — Tyler sitting on the bench beside it, saying nothing, staying.

**GHOST (TEXT CARD, mid-phrase)**
I'm not really a ghost.

**TYLER**
I didn't ask.

**GHOST (TEXT CARD)**
Nobody ever does, until they do.

The piano continues under the scene change.

## SCENE SIX — UNTRANSLATED

```
EXT. WHEREVER THE TREE ALREADY WAS — CONTINUOUS, NO CAMERA MOVEMENT
```
