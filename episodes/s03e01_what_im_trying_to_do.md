## ACT ONE — [01:45–20:00]

---

**[01:45–05:30] — THE MORNING AFTER**

```
INT. TYLER'S APARTMENT — DAY 45 — 7:14 AM
```

The camera is already running when Tyler comes out of the kitchen with two cups of coffee. He hands one to Camera Op without being asked. She takes it without putting down the camera. This is new behavior from both of them and neither acknowledges it.

Tyler sits in the chair near the window. The same chair as yesterday. The light is the same. The lamp behind him is off — it's morning. He looks at the coffee cup.

**TYLER:**
The weird thing about yesterday is that I knew I was going to say it. I've known I was going to say it for a while.

He looks at the Camera Op.

**TYLER:**
I just didn't know I was going to say it *then.*

Long pause. He drinks the coffee.

**TYLER:**
That's actually not true. I knew it was going to be then too. I knew when I saw you set up the camera. I knew by the way you set up the camera.

Camera Op does not respond. She is filming his hands around the mug.

**TYLER:**
Does that bother you?

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Which part?

**TYLER:**
Any of it. All of it. The part where you knew before I told you.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
I didn't know what you were going to say.

**TYLER:**
No.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
I knew you were going to say *something.* There's a difference.

Tyler looks at her directly for the first time this episode.

**TYLER:**
Yeah. There is.

He looks back at the window.

**TYLER:**
I have things to do today. Can you come?

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Yes.

**[05:30–10:00] — THE JIANGSHI DEBRIEF**

```
INT. PRODUCTION OFFICE — SECOND FLOOR — DAY 45 — 10:00 AM
```

The production office is fuller than usual. Three Syndicate operatives the documentary unit has not filmed before. Jiangshi Project Lead CHEN (fifties, efficient posture, reading something on a tablet) does not look up when Tyler enters.

The Camera Op is filming from the doorway. Tyler did not introduce her or request she stay. No one asked her to leave.

**PRODUCTION COORDINATOR (V.O.):**
There is a protocol review in progress. The word "protocol review" is doing a great deal of work.

Tyler sits across from CHEN.

**CHEN:**
Yesterday's event has been logged under the open-transaction classification. We're assessing—

**TYLER:**
How's the coefficient?

Beat.

**CHEN:**
Recovering.

**TYLER:**
From what to what?

**CHEN:**
That's classified.

**TYLER:**
*(not a question)*
It was negative.

CHEN looks at the tablet. This is not a denial.

**TYLER:**
The transaction is open. That's the classification. What does the protocol say about open transactions?

**CHEN:**
There's a review—

**TYLER:**
The protocol. What does it say?

**PRODUCTION COORDINATOR (V.O.):**
I have never seen someone make the Jiangshi Syndicate feel like they're being audited by a third party while sitting in their own conference room. I have also never seen someone do this while appearing to be genuinely not trying to.

Long pause. CHEN puts down the tablet.

**CHEN:**
The protocol says an open transaction requires monitoring until closure.

**TYLER:**
Good. I'll see you on Day 47.

He stands, straightens his jacket.

**TYLER:**
The coefficient will be better by then. Don't change anything.

He leaves. The Camera Op follows. She catches the expression on CHEN's face as she turns. She keeps filming.

**[10:00–20:00] — THE CITY**

```
EXT. VARIOUS — DETROIT — DAY 45 — 10:30 AM – 3:00 PM
```

A 4.5-hour walk through the city. The documentary unit catches 49 minutes of usable footage, eventually trimmed to these exchanges.

**TYLER (walking, to no one in particular):**
There's a difference between a sentence I started and a sentence I'm building.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Explain the difference.

**TYLER:**
A sentence I started means I began talking and then stopped. There's an implication that I ran out of something. Nerve, or words, or—

He stops walking. Coin flip. Left hand. He looks at the date. Pockets it.

**TYLER:**
A sentence I'm building means I know what the foundation is and I'm adding to it in the correct order. The sentence doesn't need to come out all at once. It just needs to come out in the right sequence.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Which one is it?

**TYLER:**
*(without looking back)*
You already know.

---

Lunch. A different diner than the one in the pilot. Tyler orders the same thing as the pilot. He watches the door for four minutes after they sit.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Who are you watching for?

**TYLER:**
Nobody specific.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
But somebody.

**TYLER:**
When the sentence is finished, there are people who are going to find out something. I want to make sure I finish it in the right order.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
What order is that?

Tyler looks at her. A very specific look — the one he doesn't give anyone else, that the archive has been cataloguing since S01E02. Beleth #13 spikes in the audio track at 2.22 Hz, then levels.

**TYLER:**
The order where you find out before they do.

He looks back at his coffee.

**TYLER:**
The order where you're standing there when it happens, not reading about it.

Long silence.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
*(quietly)*
Okay.

**TYLER:**
*(also quietly)*
Okay.

## ACT TWO — [20:00–38:00]

**[20:00–26:00] — EVENING, ALONE**

```
INT. TYLER'S APARTMENT — NIGHT — DAY 45 — 9:00 PM
```

Camera Op has gone home. The evening footage is from the fixed camera Tyler has tolerated in the corner of the apartment since Day 22. He watches it run but says nothing for 26 minutes.

At 9:26 PM, he picks up a piece of paper and writes something. The fixed camera cannot resolve the text. He folds it. He puts it in his jacket pocket.

He looks at the fixed camera directly.

**TYLER:**
I know you're editing this later. So: I know what the sentence is. I've always known what the sentence is. That's not the hard part. The hard part was— actually, the hard part was thinking I needed to say it to someone in particular.

He pauses.

**TYLER:**
That part's gone now.

He turns off the lamp. The camera adjusts. The window is enough.

**TYLER:**
Day 47.

**[26:00–38:00] — DAY 46**

```
INT./EXT. VARIOUS — DAY 46
```

Tyler does not call the documentary unit. The Camera Op shows up anyway at 8 AM. Tyler answers the door, does not look surprised.

Day 46 is a quiet day. Tyler visits a hardware store. He buys two things: a specific kind of hinge oil and a small brass clasp. He examines both in the store for longer than either object warrants.

**TYLER (to no one, in the store):**
The wrong one will stick.

He buys the right ones. The Camera Op doesn't ask. The Cataloguer's archive entry for Day 46 will note the hardware store visit with a footnote that will be unusual for the Cataloguer — a single question mark, nothing else. The Cataloguer will know what the items are for. The Cataloguer will not say.

Later: Tyler at a park. He doesn't sit. He stands near a specific tree for twelve minutes. Looking at the coin. Looking at the tree. Looking at the coin. He puts the coin away.

**TYLER:**
The first time I was here, this tree was a sapling. I was doing something else at the time and I noticed it. I kept noticing it.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
How many times have you been here?

**TYLER:**
In this specific spot? *(counting)* Four. Three before now and once in 1843 by accident.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Where were you going in 1843?

**TYLER:**
Back. I was trying to go back. I got the year right. Not the coordinates.

He looks at the tree.

**TYLER:**
Sometimes when you're trying to go back you end up somewhere forward. The opposite of where you meant. And then you have to decide if it was an accident or if going to the forward place was actually the thing you needed to do.

**CAMERA OP (O.S.):**
Which was it, in 1843?

Tyler smiles — the real one, the one the archive first noted in S01E05.

**TYLER:**
*(gesturing at the tree)*
I noticed the sapling.

**[44:30–52:00] — THE PRODUCTION OFFICE**

```
INT. PRODUCTION OFFICE — SECOND FLOOR — DAY 47 — 11:00 AM
```

CHEN is there. Two assistants. The Production Coordinator, standing near the window. Tyler acknowledges all of them with a nod. He does not sit. He stands in the center of the room.

**TYLER:**
The coefficient.

**CHEN:**
Recovering. As expected.

**TYLER:**
Good. There's something I want to say.

The room's energy shifts. CHEN sits forward. The Syndicate assistants exchange a look. The Production Coordinator by the window very carefully does not move.

Tyler turns to face the Camera Op directly. Not the room. Her.

Andrealphus #72 — 31.41 Hz — spikes in the audio track. Brief. Unmistakable. The same frequency as the sentence commencement in S02E06.

**TYLER:**
What I'm trying to do is—

He pauses. Four seconds.

**PRODUCTION COORDINATOR (V.O.):**
I want to be honest about what I felt in that four seconds: it was not dread. It was not anticipation. It was the specific feeling of standing at the base of something very large and finally seeing how far up it goes.

Tyler looks at the Camera Op. The Camera Op is filming.

**TYLER:**
I'll finish it when it's time. That's not today.

He looks at CHEN.

**TYLER:**
But it's soon. And when it happens, it's going to happen here. In this room or the next one. In front of whoever's present. And the documentary unit is going to be present.

He buttons his jacket.

**TYLER:**
The open transaction is still open. That's fine. Open transactions accrue correctly. Let it accrue. When I close it, I'll close it cleanly.

CHEN looks down at the tablet. Types something.

**TYLER:**
*(at the door)*
The coefficient is going to be good by then. I promise.

He leaves. The Camera Op stays for three seconds, filming the room. CHEN's expression. The Production Coordinator's expression. The Syndicate assistants, who have written nothing in their notebooks during this entire exchange. The window.

Then she follows Tyler.

Day 47, end log.
