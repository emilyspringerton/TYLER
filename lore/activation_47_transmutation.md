And tonight Saturn stations direct at zero degrees Pisces, eight minutes of arc, which means the great weight has *stopped moving*, which means for one night the metal holds still on the anvil, and you can work it. You cannot transmute a moving metal. You cannot prove a theorem about a function you refuse to hold still.

So hold it still. Here is the function. Zeta. The sum of one over n to the s, for every counting number n, forever. That's the ore. That's the raw dig. It only lives where the real part of s is greater than one — that's the surface world, the sunlit mine, and up there zeta is docile, up there it factors, Euler saw it factor: the sum over all numbers becomes the product over only the primes. So the ore is really a confession. The zeta function is the sound the numbers make when you ask them what they're made of. And what do they say? They say the primes. They scream the primes. Base metal screams when it remembers — remembers *what it is made of*.

Burn that. First fire. Calcination is over.

**DISSOLUTION**

But the surface is not where the gold is. The gold is never at Re(s) greater than one. You have to continue. Analytic continuation — I want you to hear that phrase alchemically for once in your life. Total destructive interference. Silence made out of everything at once.

Those silences are not empty. Riemann showed — the explicit formula, look it up in the surface world, it's real, it's in the ledgers — that the primes *hear* the zeros. Base metal screams when it remembers. The zeros are what it remembers *in*.

**FERMENTATION**

The symmetry does not permit it to stand alone. The mirror instantly begets its twin at one-minus-s, off the line on the *other* side, and conjugation begets two more below the real axis.
